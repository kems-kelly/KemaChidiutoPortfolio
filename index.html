MOBILE DESIGN PROTOTYPING Framer

KoloFunds Save Strictly, Live Freely

NaliTech Co • UX Engineer

CHALLENGE

Many young earners struggle with saving consistently. Traditional saving apps felt complicated, lacked motivation, and didn’t create a sense of progress or reward. Users wanted a simple, friendly way to build financial discipline.

SOLUTION

Designed KoloFunds as a simple, goal-based savings platform that makes saving effortless. Focused on clean visuals, quick setup, and gentle nudges to encourage habit formation. The result an intuitive experience that helps users save strictly and stay consistent without overthinking it.

WEB DESIGN PROTOTYPING React

Health Axis – Hospital Management System

Product Designer • UX Engineer

CHALLENGE

Hospital staff struggled with scattered patient data, slow record updates, and inefficient communication between departments. Manual workflows caused delays in patient care and administrative errors.

SOLUTION

Designed a centralized digital platform that streamlines hospital operations — from patient registration to billing and doctor scheduling. Focused on an intuitive dashboard layout, role-based access, and clean data visualization. Built interactive prototypes to validate usability with medical staff before development.
